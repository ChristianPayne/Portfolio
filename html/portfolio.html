Genesis Motors USA

About The App

The Genesis Wheelstands were developed to add an interactive experience to the auto show floor. These apps had all the content needed to make sure the customer was well informed about the car they were looking at. There are a total of 5 different cars inside the same app that can be selected depending on what car the app is in front of.

Development

The development team behind the interactive experience was Related Grey and ARTS4ALL (A4A). The wheelstands were made using Unity3D and deployed on Surface tablets.
